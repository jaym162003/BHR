Balicaocao Eco-Park, also known as Balicaocao Highland Resort, is located at Barangay Orong,Kabankalan City. It is nestled on the slope of the mountain 500 feet above sea level which gives you a panoramic view of the lowlands, the City of Kabankalan and the southern part of Negros Occidental.
